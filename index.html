The Rally in California is the first of an annual end-of-summer event for students from 6th-12th grade. To outreach to them to start their relationship with God through accepting Christ as their savior and get any young person who is not currently plugged into a youth group plugged in. We also want it to be a kickoff for the school year for those already in a youth group and a way to provide hope in a turbulent world. It is a time to have unity amongst young people, youth groups, and churches that we are united in giving the gospel of Christ that provides salvation and hope for this world.
